# 📋 DENR Scheduler / Planner - User Guide

Welcome to the **DENR Scheduler / Planner**! This guide provides a comprehensive walkthrough of the application's features and how to effectively manage your office's schedule.

---

## 🌟 Overview
The DENR Scheduler is a centralized platform designed for the Department of Environment and Natural Resources - CAR. It allows different offices and divisions to coordinate activities, prevent scheduling conflicts, and maintain a historical record of official events.

---

## 📅 Navigating the Dashboard
The main dashboard is your primary tool for viewing the regional schedule.

-   **Dynamic Calendar**: Events are displayed in a monthly grid. Click on any date to see all activities scheduled for that day.
-   **Color Coding**: Events are categorized by type (e.g., Meetings, Workshops, Travel) with distinct colors for quick identification.
-   **Office Filtering**: Use the **Select Office** dropdown to view events for a specific division or service.
-   **Category Filtering**: Use the **Category** dropdown to filter by activity type.
-   **Upcoming Events**: The sidebar on the right lists the most recent upcoming activities across all offices.

---

## ➕ Managing Events

### 1. Creating a New Event
*Requires login with Office credentials.*
1.  Click the **Add Event** button on your dashboard.
2.  **Category & Title**: Choose a category and provide a clear, descriptive title.
3.  **Date Selection**:
    *   **Single Day**: Choose a specific date.
    *   **Date Range**: For multi-day workshops or travel, select a start and end date.
4.  **Time**: Specify the start and end times.
5.  **Participants**:
    *   Select participating divisions or services.
    *   Click on a division name to pick specific employees from that office.
6.  **Attachments**: Upload PDF programs, invitations, or other relevant documents.
7.  **Save**: Click "Add Event" to publish. An email notification will be sent automatically.

### 2. Editing or Deleting Events
-   You can only modify events created by your own office.
-   Click an event in the calendar to open its details.
-   If you have permission, **Edit** and **Delete** buttons will appear at the bottom of the modal.
-   *Note: Past or archived events cannot be edited.*

---

## 🗄️ Archive System
Past events are never lost. The system automatically moves completed activities to the **Archive** every 10 minutes.
-   Access the archive via the **Archive** link in the top navigation bar.
-   Use the same filtering tools to look up historical data or past meeting participants.

---

## 📱 Mobile & Tablet Features
The application is fully responsive and optimized for use on smartphones and tablets.
-   **Portrait Mode**: The layout automatically stacks elements vertically for easier scrolling.
-   **Smart Abbreviations**: On small screens, long names like "Licenses, Patents and Deeds Division" are shortened to **LPDD** to keep the interface clean.
-   **Touch-Friendly**: All calendar cells and badges are designed for easy tapping.

## ❓ Troubleshooting
-   **Cannot Edit Event**: Ensure you are logged in and that the event was created by your office.
-   **Logo Not Loading**: Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.
-   **Time Not Updating**: The header clock updates every minute; if it seems stuck, check your device's system time.
